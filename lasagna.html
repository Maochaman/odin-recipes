Lasagna

Lasagna is a type of pasta, possibly one of the oldest types, made of very wide, flat sheets.

Ingredients

  • Tomatos
  • Onion and garlic
  • lasagna noodles
  • Cheeses

Steps

  1. Meat sauce
  2. noodles
  3. Ricotta mixture
  4. Mozarella slices
  5. Meat sauce
  6. Parmesan cheese
  7. Repeat the layers then top with the remaining Parmesan
